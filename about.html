Revolutionizing Campus Dining

Canteen Companion is a web-based food ordering application designed to modernize college canteens by replacing inefficient manual processes with a digital workflow.

The Problem

We noticed that college canteens suffer from long queues, manual order errors, and slow cash handling. This overcrowding often leads to wasted academic time for students and faculty.

Our Solution

We built a platform enabling online menu viewing, remote ordering, and seamless digital payments (UPI). Our goal is to streamline operations for canteen owners while saving precious break time for students.

Key Innovation: Virtual Waiting

Our defining feature is "Virtual Waiting"‚Äîshifting the wait from physical lines to a digital space. This allows students to study or relax while their food is prepared, notifying them only when it is ready for pickup.

Our Impact

Operational Efficiency

Drastically reduces waiting times and manual workload while increasing order processing speed

Transparent Transactions

Ensures accurate financial tracking and minimizes revenue leakage through secure digital payments.

Student Centric

Designed for high-density college environments to solve the unavoidable daily need for efficient food service.
